In the expanding world of botanical extracts, few ingredients command the respect and scientific attention of *Panax notoginseng*. Known in traditional Chinese medicine as Sanqi or Tianqi, this remarkable plant has been a cornerstone of health for centuries. Today, its standardized extract—specifically the fraction known as **[Notoginseng](https://www.qherb.net/product/notoginseng-total-saponins/)** Total Saponins—is gaining significant traction in the global nutraceutical and pharmaceutical industries.

For formulators and product developers, understanding the value of this extract, particularly when sourced from reputable Chinese suppliers, is key to unlocking its full potential.

## What is Panax Notoginseng Extract?

*Panax notoginseng* belongs to the same genus as the famous Panax ginseng. The name "Panax" itself derives from the Greek word for "cure-all," hinting at its broad therapeutic reputation [](https://www.biowayorganicinc.com/organic-plant-extract/panax-notoginseng-extract.html). The extract is typically derived from the dried roots, rootlets, and rhizomes of the plant [](https://hmc.usp.org/printpdf/monographs/panax-notoginseng-root-and-rhizome-dry-extract-1-0#1#1)[](https://hmc.usp.org/monograph/panax-notoginseng-root-and-rhizome-dry-extract). What sets it apart is its unique composition of active compounds, primarily a group of saponins known as notoginsenosides.

### The Power of Standardization: Total Saponins

The term "Notoginseng Total Saponins" refers to the concentrated fraction of these active, steroid-like compounds [](https://www.biowayorganicinc.com/organic-plant-extract/panax-notoginseng-extract.html). The primary active constituents include Notoginsenoside R1, Ginsenoside Rg1, Ginsenoside Re, Ginsenoside Rb1, and Ginsenoside Rd [](http://www.qualityherb.net/product/Notoginseng-total-taponins.html)[](https://www.biowayorganicinc.com/organic-plant-extract/panax-notoginseng-extract.html)[](https://www.kingsci.com/nutraceutical-ingredients/china-panax-notoginseng-root-extract.html).

Reputable suppliers offer extracts standardized to high levels of total saponins, often ranging from 50% to 80% or higher [](https://www.biowayorganicinc.com/organic-plant-extract/panax-notoginseng-extract.html)[](https://www.kingsci.com/nutraceutical-ingredients/china-panax-notoginseng-root-extract.html). A specification for "Total Saponins ≥80%" is a common industry benchmark, ensuring a consistent and potent product with every batch [](https://www.kingsci.com/nutraceutical-ingredients/china-panax-notoginseng-root-extract.html)[](https://hmc.usp.org/monograph/panax-notoginseng-root-and-rhizome-dry-extract).

## The Science-Backed Benefits

The interest in this extract is fueled by a robust body of scientific literature confirming its wide-ranging pharmacological activities [](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1211/jpp.58.8.0001). Here are the key areas where it shines:

### A Champion for Cardiovascular Health

The primary driver for using Notoginseng Total Saponins is its profound effect on the cardiovascular system. Research highlights several mechanisms [](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1211/jpp.58.8.0001):

* **Cardioprotection:** It has been shown to reduce myocardial oxygen consumption, protect myocardial cells from damage, and enhance their antioxidant capacity [](https://www.gaoyuanbio.com/natural-ingredients/botanic-extracts/panax-notoginseng-extract-powder.html).
* **Blood Circulation:** It helps to inhibit platelet aggregation and has antithrombotic and anticoagulant effects, promoting healthy blood flow [](http://www.qualityherb.net/product/Notoginseng-total-taponins.html)[](https://www.gaoyuanbio.com/natural-ingredients/botanic-extracts/panax-notoginseng-extract-powder.html).
* **Vascular Health:** Studies indicate it can dilate cerebral vessels, increase cerebral blood flow, and help lower blood lipid levels [](http://www.qualityherb.net/product/Notoginseng-total-taponins.html)[](https://www.gaoyuanbio.com/natural-ingredients/botanic-extracts/panax-notoginseng-extract-powder.html).

### Emerging Research: Metabolic Health and Beyond

The benefits of P. notoginseng extend beyond the cardiovascular system. A recent 2022 study found that both the ethanolic extract and the total saponins (PNS) can help regulate lipid profiles and alleviate fatty liver in obese mice. They achieved this by reducing triglyceride, total cholesterol, and LDL-cholesterol levels [](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/m/pubmed.16418439/35718787/?i=36&from=/19107746/related)[](https://ouci.dntb.gov.ua/en/works/4KXkbY3l/). This suggests a potential role in managing metabolic health [](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/m/pubmed.16418439/35718787/?i=36&from=/19107746/related).

### Other Notable Activities

The diverse saponin profile contributes to other effects, including:

* **Anti-inflammatory and Antioxidant:** It acts as a powerful agent to combat oxidative stress and inflammation .
* **Hepatoprotective and Renoprotective:** It supports liver and kidney health .
* **Hemostatic Properties:** It is also valued for its ability to help stop bleeding.
